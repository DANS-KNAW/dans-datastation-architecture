NBN Resolver¶

The NBN resolver service is operated by the Koninklijke Bibliotheek. It resolves NBN persistent identifiers to their current location. The resolver is hosted at https://persistent-identifier.nl/.

The DANS Data Vault registers the long-term preservation copies of datasets with the NBN resolver through its API.
