DataverseNL¶

The differences between DataverseNL and the Data Stations are mainly contractual and organisational. In DataverseNL, the customer organization is responsible for the curation of datasets. The technical differences are minimal:

  • DataverseNL has been configured with multiple collections, each of which is administered by a customer organization. The Data Stations have a single collection per Data Station, which is administered by DANS.
  • DataverseNL has no SWORD2 service.
